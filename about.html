About Me

As a highly experienced leader of multidisciplinary teams, I lead projects in development, data, infrastructure, and compliance, ensuring efficient and effective project delivery. I possess the ability to establish OKRs, monitor applications, conduct code reviews, and handle software architecture, while also directly managing data integrity and lifecycle, preserving privacy and security, and supporting project managers and PO's, developers, infrastructure and support teams, and documenting software and established processes.

I have also played a key role in implementing and maintaining ISO27001 & ISO27701 standards, ensuring the security and compliance of company-wide data. I conduct cost analysis for cloud services (Azure, AWS, Digital Ocean) and annual budgeting, always striving for the best combination of cost efficiency and performance. I hold meetings with executive management, implement processes such as GTD, Agile methodology, and management tools, offering innovative and customized solutions for each project.

In my management style, I strive to maintain a more human approach, focusing on developing all teams under my leadership, always considering the happiness and well-being of my colleagues. My goal is always to achieve high performance results while maintaining a healthy and collaborative work environment.
